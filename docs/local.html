Getting to Toronto

By Air

Toronto is served by Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ), the largest airport in Canada, with connections from across North America and internationally. Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ) also serves select domestic and US routes.

By Train

Via Rail serves Toronto’s Union Station, with connections from Montreal, Ottawa, Québec City, Windsor, and Niagara Falls.

Getting Around Toronto

Toronto has an extensive transit system operated by the TTC (Toronto Transit Commission), including subway, streetcar, and bus routes. The PRESTO card is the easiest way to pay for transit.

Things to Do in Toronto

Toronto offers world-class museums, restaurants, and attractions:

  • CN Tower — iconic Toronto landmark and observation tower
  • Royal Ontario Museum — art, culture, and natural history
  • Art Gallery of Ontario — one of Canada’s largest art museums
  • Kensington Market — eclectic neighbourhood with food, shops, and street art
  • St. Lawrence Market — historic market with local vendors
  • Toronto Islands — ferry ride to parks and beaches with city views
